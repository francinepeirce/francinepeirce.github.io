About Me

Thank you for visiting my site and taking a moment to learn more about me! I was born in the smallest state in the United States on a chilly winter morning sometime in the late 1980s. I currently work for Amazon as a DOT Certified Driver Trainer and have been an Amazonian for nearly 4 years. I formerly worked as a customer service representative for Amazon, and also have several years of customer service experience with other companies before boarding the Amazon ship. I also interned as a radio DJ at a classic hits station in my home state of Rhode Island when I was 19; it was the career path I wanted to follow at the time, but it never worked out for me due to job cuts in the industry. However, I feel the skills I acquired during that time have been and will continue to be beneficial in my future endeavors.
